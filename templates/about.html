How to Pepe?

1. Every week, artists submit digital art to the gallery

2. People in the Rare Pepe community vote on which art to accept

3. Two pieces of art are chosen each week

4. The first piece is chosen by weighted majority vote of owned Pepecash

5. The second piece is chosen by weighted majority vote of owned Pepe cards

6. After the week is over, art must be resubmitted to compete again

7. Details (subject to significant change) can be found at the docs here

8. Please note: Art submissions, voting, and vote delegation should all be fully functional. Try them out!
